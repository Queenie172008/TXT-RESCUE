About

TXT-RESCUE by GROUP 5 GALILEI

TXT-RESCUE – Group 5 Galilei is a disaster relief initiative dedicated to utilizing SMS brigades and internet platforms for rapid emergency response. Our project focuses on providing real-time disaster alerts, mobilizing volunteers, and ensuring transparent relief distribution through digital communication. By bridging the gap between affected communities and responders, we aim to enhance disaster preparedness, coordination, and recovery efforts. With technology as our tool and resilience as our goal, we are committed to making every second count in times of crisis.

We Have More You

Driven by innovation and compassion, we work alongside local authorities, organizations, and community members to enhance preparedness and response efforts. Our team believes that timely information saves lives, and through technology, we can empower people to take immediate action in times of crisis. Together, we stand committed to making disaster relief faster, smarter, and more accessible to everyone.
